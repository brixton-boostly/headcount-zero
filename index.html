FAQ

Questions we hear in every eval

How long does setup actually take?

Most teams are up and running in under an hour. Connect your integrations, configure your first workflow, invite your team. No implementation project. No dedicated CSM required.

Do I need engineering to set up integrations?

No. All integrations are OAuth-based — you click "Connect," authorize, and you're done. No API keys, no engineering tickets.

Can I start with just one module?

Yes, and we recommend it. Pick the workflow that's hurting most, get that working, then expand when you're ready. The Starter plan is designed for exactly this.

What if my team resents another tool?

Headcount Zero lives inside Slack and email — it meets your team where they already work. Managers interact with it through Slack DMs. No new login, no new dashboard for them to ignore.

Is my data secure?

SOC 2 Type II certified. Data encrypted at rest and in transit. We never train models on your data. Full data processing agreement available before you sign up.

What happens after the free trial?

You pick a plan or you walk away — no auto-charge, no surprise invoice. If you need more time, just ask. We'd rather you be sure.
