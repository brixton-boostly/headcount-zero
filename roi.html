How we calculate these estimates

We use conservative multipliers based on aggregated data from our customers (50–500 employees). Your actual results may vary. We'd rather under-promise.

Hiring ops

We estimate 8 hrs of ops overhead per open role (scheduling, coordination, pipeline updates). Headcount Zero reduces this to 2.5 hrs per role. Savings = open_roles × 5.5 hrs / 3 per month.

Performance reviews

Your input for quarterly hours spent on review admin. Headcount Zero automates nudges, tracking, and compilation — reducing admin time by 90%. Savings = quarterly_hours × 0.9 / 3 per month.

Comp benchmarking

We estimate 5% of employees have a comp conversation per quarter. Each conversation without data wastes ~1.5 hrs in back-and-forth. With benchmark data, you save 1 hr per guesswork conversation. Savings = employees × 0.05 × guesswork_pct × 1 hr / 3 per month.

Compliance

Base compliance overhead is 3 hrs/mo for one state, plus 2 hrs/mo per additional state (policy reviews, handbook updates, tracking law changes). Headcount Zero reduces this to 0.5 hrs/mo total. Savings = 3 + (states - 1) × 2 - 0.5 hrs/mo.

Onboarding & offboarding

Hires/quarter approximated by open roles. Departures estimated at 4% of employees per quarter. Current overhead: 4 hrs per onboard, 3 hrs per offboard. With HCZ: 1 hr and 0.5 hrs. Savings calculated per month.

Dollar values

All hours valued at $75/hr (blended ops team cost). HCZ cost assumes the Growth plan at $12/employee/month. Payback = months until savings exceed HCZ cost.
